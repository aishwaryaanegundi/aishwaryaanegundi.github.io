About Me

From Code to Intelligence

I began my career as an Application Developer at ThoughtWorks — one of the world's foremost software consultancies — where I honed the craft of clean code, test-driven development, and agile engineering. Working on biometric data systems planted the seed for what would become a deep passion for machine learning.

That curiosity led me to pursue a Master's degree at Saarland University (Germany), specialising in Deep Learning and NLP within the Embedded Systems programme. Alongside my studies I joined the Max Planck Institute of Informatics as a Research Assistant, where I work on Social Media Analysis — building models that surface meaningful patterns from the vast, noisy stream of human discourse online.

I'm drawn to problems that sit at the boundary of language and intelligence: systems that don't just process text, but genuinely comprehend it. My aim is to bring both rigorous research thinking and production engineering discipline to every project I work on.
